Frequently asked questions

What is Cowork?

Cowork is a gamified, virtual coworking space. Students, colleagues, and freelance friends can use it to stay motivated and get work done together in cozy rooms they design.

Is Cowork free?

The fundamental features of Cowork are available for all members to use, and I'm planning on keeping it that way. To keep the servers open, I am developing a Pro version for those who'd like more cosmetic options and advanced features. To learn more, check out the pricing page. Since Cowork began as a small hobby project and is still maintained by just one person (hi!), I'd really appreciate your support.

How can I download the Cowork app?

Cowork isn't on app stores just yet, but it does have a great Progressive Web App (PWA) you can download. It looks and feels just like an app you'd find on an app store, and it's available on iOS and Android. Learn how to install the Cowork app here.

Who makes Cowork?

Cowork is a project by Innkeeper Games, a one-person indie game studio. Development on Cowork began in April 2020 (when schools closed during the COVID-19 pandemic) as a way for me, its creator, to cowork with friends and stay accountable.
