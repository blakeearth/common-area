Install the Cowork app on iOS

The Cowork Progressive Web App (PWA) looks and feels like an ordinary app.

Tap the "share" button at the bottom-center of Safari on any Cowork page.

Tap "Add to Home Screen".

Press "Add" in the top right corner.

That's it! Launch the app from your home screen.

Install the Cowork app on Android

We don't have a tutorial for this yet, but check out this resource on installing PWAs on Android devices.
